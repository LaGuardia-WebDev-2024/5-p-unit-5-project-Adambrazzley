Nintendo has revolutionized gaming over decades. Here's a look at some of their iconic consoles.

Console Release Year Notable Features

  • 8-bit graphics
  • Cartridge games
  • Classics like Super Mario Bros.
GameCube 2001
  • Mini-DVDs
  • Compact design
  • Games like Super Smash Bros.
Nintendo Switch 2017
  • Hybrid console
  • Joy-Con controllers
  • Games like The Legend of Zelda
